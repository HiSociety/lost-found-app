Community Guidelines

Our guidelines ensure a safe and effective platform for everyone.

Document Posting Guidelines

1. Verify Document Authenticity

Ensure the document is genuine before posting. Check for official seals and security features.

2. Protect Personal Information

Only share necessary details. Avoid posting sensitive information like full ID numbers or personal addresses.

3. Clear Photos

Upload clear, well-lit photos that show the document type while obscuring sensitive details.

Safe Handover Practices

1. Public Meeting Places

Always meet in well-lit, public locations like police stations or government offices.

2. Verify Identity

Ask for additional proof of identity before handing over documents.

3. Document the Handover

Keep a record of the handover, including date, time, and location.

Community Behavior

1. Respectful Communication

Be polite and professional in all interactions. Avoid sharing personal contact details publicly.

2. No Monetary Rewards

Our platform prohibits requesting or offering monetary rewards for returning documents.

3. Report Issues

Report any suspicious behavior or inappropriate content to our moderation team.

Rating System

1. Fair Ratings

Rate others based on their helpfulness, communication, and adherence to guidelines.

2. Honest Testimonials

Share your experience to help build trust in our community. Be truthful and constructive.

3. Helper Points

Earn points by helping others and maintaining a high rating. Points contribute to your community status.
